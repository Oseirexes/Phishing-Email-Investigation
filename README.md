# Phishing-Email-Investigation
This project documents the investigation of a phishing email as part of my SOC Analyst and Digital Forensics learning journey. The objective was to analyze a suspicious email, identify indicators of compromise (IOCs), determine the phishing techniques used, and document the findings using an industry-style investigation report.

Investigation Objectives:
Analyze a suspicious phishing email.
Decode Base64-encoded content using CyberChef.
Extract embedded attachments.
Identify Indicators of Compromise (IOCs).
Assess the threat posed by the email.
Document findings and provide recommendations.

Investigation Process

The investigation followed a structured SOC analyst workflow:

Reviewed the email content and metadata.
Examined the email headers for sender information and routing.
Used CyberChef to decode the Base64-encoded attachment.
Extracted the embedded PDF, JPEG, and Excel files.
Identified suspicious artifacts and potential IOCs.
Determined the phishing technique and potential impact.
Documented findings, evidence, and recommendations.
